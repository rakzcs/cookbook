# Italian Broccoli Parmesan Soup

A comforting soup combining broccoli and Parmesan with a hint of spice.

## Ingredients

- Broccoli: 1 head
- Yellow onion, chopped: 1 (medium-sized)
- Garlic cloves: 3
- Olive oil: 3 tbsp
- Butter: 2 knobs
- Garlic powder: 1 tbsp
- Salt: 1 tbsp
- Red pepper flakes (optional): 1 tsp
- Fresh thyme: 1 sprig
- Stock (vegetable, chicken, or beef): 1 liter (about 4 cups)
- Water: 2 cups (475 ml)
- Small pasta (orzo, ditalini, or anelli siciliani): 1 cup
- Lemon: 1/2 (use more if desired)
- Parmesan cheese, shaved
- Black pepper, to taste
- Optional herbs for garnish

## Instructions

1. Separate broccoli florets from the stem. Cut florets and stems into small pieces.
2. Chop the onion and garlic.
3. In a large pot, heat olive oil and butter over medium heat. Add chopped onion, stirring for 2 minutes.
4. Add broccoli stems, cook for 2 minutes, then add florets and garlic. Stir.
5. Season with garlic powder, salt, and red pepper flakes. Add more butter if needed. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
6. Add stock and water. Bring to a gentle simmer, add thyme, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until broccoli is tender.
7. Lightly blend the soup using a potato masher or immersion blender for texture.
8. Add pasta, increase heat to medium, and cook as per package directions.
9. Finish with lemon juice, shaved Parmesan, optional herbs, olive oil, and black pepper.
