## Mushroom Pasta

Link: [Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=XVJB764Y1Uc)

**Ingredients:**
- 200g mushrooms
- Approximately 10 cloves of garlic
- 1 onion
- 1 tablespoon butter
- 240ml broth
- 1 tablespoon oregano
- 80ml heavy cream
- 80ml grated Parmesan cheese
- Parsley

**Instructions:**

1. Slice the mushrooms into thick slices.
2. Chop most of the garlic cloves into small pieces.
3. Chop the onion and parsley.
4. Heat olive oil in a pan over low heat.
5. Crush 2 garlic cloves and add them to the oil.
6. Add the onion to the pan and when it becomes soft, add the butter.
7. Add the mushrooms and cook for 10-15 minutes.
8. Add the remaining chopped garlic when the mushrooms turn brown and continue cooking.
9. Add the broth, salt, and pepper.
10. Add dried oregano and some parsley. Let it simmer for 10 minutes to blend the flavors.
11. Add the cream and mix well.
12. Grate the Parmesan cheese and add it to the sauce. Stir until it becomes creamy.
13. Add the remaining parsley.
14. Taste and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
